ABOUT ME

Xian-Ji Kuang is an Assistant Professor of Finance at National Changhua University of Education. His research interests include empirical asset pricing, machine learning in asset pricing, financial technology (FinTech), financial engineering, and high-dimensional data analysis. He has published in the International Review of Economics and Finance, Computational Economics, Applied Economics Letters, and Applied Economics.
